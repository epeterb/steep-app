## Why This Matters

### The Problem We're Solving
People save great LinkedIn content but:
- Never see it again after the weekly email
- Can't search through past saves
- Have no way to organize by topic
- Lose valuable insights over time
- Can't build on what they've learned

### The Breakthrough
Steep transforms LinkedIn from a **consumption platform** into a **learning system**:

1. **Capture** - Forward posts without breaking flow
2. **Organize** - AI tags & collections auto-categorize
3. **Search** - Find any insight in seconds
4. **Learn** - "Ask Your Library" synthesizes answers
5. **Share** - Public collections become growth engine

## Marketing Angles

### The Pitch Evolution

**Week 1 (Newsletter pitch):**
"Get a weekly digest of your saved LinkedIn posts"
→ Response: "Meh, I already get too many emails"

**Week 4 (Library pitch):**
"Build a searchable library of LinkedIn's best content"
→ Response: "Oh interesting, like Pocket for LinkedIn?"

**Week 8 (Second brain pitch):**
"Your personal knowledge base for professional learning"
→ Response: "I need this. Where do I pay?"

### Key Differentiators

vs. **Pocket/Instapaper:**
- Built for LinkedIn specifically
- AI understands business content
- Weekly synthesis, not just saving

vs. **Notion/Obsidian:**
- Zero friction capture (email forward)
- AI auto-organizes
- No setup required

vs. **Just using LinkedIn saves:**
- Can't search LinkedIn's saved posts
- No organization
- No synthesis
- Posts disappear
